Using WebX+

To use webX+ you need a WebX browser that supports custom DNS. We recommend Bussinga.

Open the settings for your browser and find the option to input custom DNS. Then enter one of the following DNS URLs, we provide 2 identical versions of our DNS to improve reliablity

  • https://webxdns.votemanager.xyz/resolve
  • https://webxdns.blocklycode.org/resolve

Then save your settings. You may need to clear your DNS cache or restart your browser as well

You can now access WebX+ domains, such as search.frontdoor, our search engine for both WebX and WebX+ domains
